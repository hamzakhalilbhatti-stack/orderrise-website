# OrderRise Stage 5.3 — Hybrid Sales + Guided 3D

## Goal

Make the website's business purpose understandable before asking visitors to interpret 3D models.

## Main message

OrderRise turns a WhatsApp customer order into coordinated restaurant work:

1. WhatsApp receives the customer request.
2. AI understands products, quantities and instructions.
3. The cart calculates items, extras and totals.
4. The kitchen receives a structured ticket.
5. Inventory reserves or reduces stock.
6. Delivery receives the fulfillment task.
7. The owner dashboard records the order, customer and revenue.

## Homepage changes
