# Real Proof Checklist

Do not publish a testimonial, logo, performance number or screen recording
until the restaurant has approved it.

For the first real pilot, collect:

- Restaurant name and written permission
- Starting order volume
- Starting missed-order estimate
- Average response time before OrderRise
- Average response time after OrderRise
- Orders handled during the pilot
- Kitchen errors before and after
- Staff time saved
- Customer feedback
- Screen recording of WhatsApp order to kitchen ticket
- Approved quote from the owner
- Permission to use logo and numbers

Then add one compact proof block after the Taco Heat demo:

- Restaurant logo
- One verified result
- One owner quote
- A 30–60 second screen recording
- Link to the full case study
