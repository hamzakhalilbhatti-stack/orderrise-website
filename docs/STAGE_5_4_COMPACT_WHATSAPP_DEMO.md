# OrderRise Stage 5.4 — Compact WhatsApp Order Demo

## Goal

Add a simple demonstration that restaurant owners can understand before they explore the larger 3D product experience.

## Demo restaurant

Taco Heat — powered by OrderRise

This is fictional demonstration information.

## Customer actions

1. Show me the menu
2. Add two zinger burgers
3. Make one spicy
4. Apply SAVE10
5. I want delivery
6. Confirm order
7. Track my order

## Live updates

- Customer chat bubbles
- Cart products and modifiers
- Coupon discount
- Delivery fee
- Final order total
- Kitchen ticket
- Inventory status
- Driver assignment
- Daily revenue
- Order progress
- Written business explanation

## Synchronization

The compact demo also updates the larger 3D order demonstration below it.
