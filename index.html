What OrderRise actually does

It turns one customer conversation into coordinated restaurant work.

01
Customer experience

Customers order without learning another app.

They browse the menu, customize food, choose pickup or delivery, confirm payment details and receive order updates inside WhatsApp.

02
Restaurant execution

Staff receive clear tasks instead of messy chat messages.

03
Owner visibility

Owners see what was ordered and what happened next.
